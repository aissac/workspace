**Files created:**
- `backend/requirements.txt` — All dependencies (FastAPI, SQLAlchemy, Celery, etc.)
- `backend/core/config.py` — Pydantic settings with environment vars
- `backend/core/database.py` — Async SQLAlchemy with connection pooling

- `backend/tests/test_api.py` — Comprehensive pytest suite
